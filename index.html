NIEUWS

Episode IV, A NEW HOPE

It is a period of civil war. Rebel spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil Galactic Empire. During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire’s ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet. Pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy….

BANDS

De volgende bands zijn bevestigd!

Up The Irons

Up The Irons is the ultimate Iron Maiden Tribute band. Unlike your regular coverband, we put on a spectacular, high-energy show complete with stage props, effects and on stage "Eddie" monsters that simulate a perfect Iron Maiden show. Combine that with note for note accuracy and musicianship and the "win-win" combination of 5 wild Iron Maiden freaks playing the original "Iron Maiden" hits and you have an unbeatable formula that guarantees a night to remember.

MOTÖRBLAST

MOTÖRBLAST is one of the most successful and most original Motörhead - imitations. Who knows Motörhead, knows what he has to expect ...... '... And do not forget the joker! ...' Supported were internationally successful acts such as, JBO, Pro Pain, Motorjesus, Tobias Regner, Zeltinger, incandescence and many more. Their shows took the band to Switzerland, Poland, Belgium with such well-known clubs like Collos-Saal in Aschaffenburg.


FEUERENGEL

Als je door RAMMSTEIN zelf uitgenodigd wordt om op hun feestje te spelen dan wordt je serieus genomen… Dat overkwam de #1 RAMMSTEIN tribute FEUERENGEL t.g.v. de uitreiking van een dubbel platina en gouden plaat voor de albums ‘Sehnsucht’ en ‘Herzeleid’. Vrijwel iedereen die ooit een RAMMSTEIN concert bezocht, kent het unieke karakter ervan. Een band die zijn eigen weg gaat. Op grond van het RAMMSTEIN concept trachtten diverse Duitse bands de weg naar roem en fame te bereiken maar wat overblijft waren de pioniers uit Berlijn. En terecht!

Pete McCall

Some say that imitation is the sincerest form of flattery. In Pete McCall’s case that is certainly true. Pete has been a massive Rod Stewart fan for longer than he cares to remember. He has attended numerous live shows by the great man from Leicester to Las Vegas. He is aware of the special atmosphere created albeit in an intimate venue of a few hundred people or a giant football stadium with an audience of thousands.Pete’s aim is to capture the spirit of the Rod Stewart live show time and time again.
